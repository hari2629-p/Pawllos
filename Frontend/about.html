About Pawllos

Pawllos is a modern pet adoption platform designed to connect abandoned or stray animals with loving families. Through the use of AI-enhanced survey matching and a clean, user-friendly interface, we make pet adoption easier, smarter, and more compassionate.

Whether you're a passionate animal lover or looking for your new furry friend, Pawllos helps match pets to your lifestyle using both your input and the pet’s behavior profile. Our platform ensures:

  • 🐾 Secure user authentication
  • 🐾 Smart pet preference matching
  • 🐾 Easy pet listing and adoption request system
  • 🐾 Personal profile management

Developed by Harigovind P. Nair, this project demonstrates how simple technologies can make big differences in the lives of animals and humans alike.
